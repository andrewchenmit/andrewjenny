How They Met

Jenny and Andrew met on July 18th, 2014 at TAO Beach, a day club in The Venetian in Las Vegas. They were both attending their class of 2011 Pi Reunion. They had never even heard of each other despite being part of the same class and same major for 4 years. Several weeks later, Andrew asked Jenny out and the rest is history!

St. Vitus Cathedral in Prague, Czech Republic.

Courtship

By August, Andrew was secretly admitting to his friends he thought he had found "the one", and Jenny had introduced her boyfriend of 2 weeks to her visiting parents. In December, the 3 month old couple spent Christmas with both families followed by 2 weeks of holiday in Central Europe. They felt truly blessed to have found such compatibility so quickly.

The proposal on Table Mountain in Cape Town, South Africa.

The Proposal

On April 10, 2016, Andrew and Jenny ascended Table Mountain, the deservedly popular scenic landmark in Cape Town. Upon finding an ideal location to view the upcoming sunset, Jenny turned around to see Andrew's family holding a big sign asking "Jenny, will you marry me?" Andrew gave a long speech, popped the question, and Jenny said yes!
